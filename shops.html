Indian Bazaar
Butala Emporium

Website: www.butalaemporium.com

Address: 37-46 74th St. Queens, NY 11372


Phone: (718) 899-5590 | Hours: 10am to 8:30pm daily

Butala Emporium was founded in 1987, based on the dream of creating a company that would provide everything India can offer. We bring you the wisdom of ancient sages through books on philosophy, in ayurveda, our mission is to provide you with the highest quality herbs and products that are pure and effective, beauty care products that are made of all natural and organic ingredients completely safe for all ages, arts and handicraft that showcases India's rich tradition and culture, fine quality stainless steel, copper and brassware that will be a part of your home for long and religious and pooja items that are essential part of yoga, meditation and Indian way of spiritual life. In our effort to bring India at your door step, our items list expands to many varieties such as incense, essential oils, henna, classical musical instruments, spiritual and meditation music, Indian festival or wedding.

GIFT SHOP
Lockwood

Website: www.lockwoodshop.com

Address: 77-13 37th Ave, Queens, NY 11372


Phone: (347) 730-6357 | Hours: 11am to 8:00pm daily

Came here for delicious pastries for breakfast, snacks, or dessert. This family-owned Uruguayan bakery specializes in Antojitos Colombianos, sweet and savory treats from Colombia and Argentina. Baked goods and sandwiches, empanadas, breads, cakes, alfajore cookies, buñuelo and breakfast foods like pan con queso are all whipped up fresh daily for patrons to take home or eat with a cup of café con leche at one of the bakery’s dozen eat-in tables. 86-10 37th Avenue, Jackson Heights, NY - Phone: 718-507-2339
